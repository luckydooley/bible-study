In their framework, relationship with God was earned through careful obedience to over 600 commandments. You climbed toward God. You worked for standing before Him.

Street Level ‚Äî The Will and the Inheritance

Imagine your estranged grandfather ‚Äî a man you've barely spoken to, maybe even disrespected, maybe even forgotten ‚Äî who dies and leaves you his entire estate. Everything. Not because you earned it. Not because you asked for it. Not even because you deserve it. Purely because he decided, while still alive, that you were worth it to him.

You didn't sign anything. You didn't agree to a repayment plan. The only thing required of you is to accept the inheritance. If you refuse or ignore it, you receive nothing ‚Äî and the consequences of that fall entirely on your own choice.

That's the structure of John 3:16. God, who owes you absolutely nothing, looked at a world that largely ignores Him, disrespects Him, or simply lives as though He doesn't exist ‚Äî and decided it was worth sending His Son into that world anyway. The entire transaction is His initiative, His cost, His gift. The only variable is you: will you receive it, or won't you?
